Friedrice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled jasmine rice (preferably a day old)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, and chopped green beans)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame oil for flavor (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro or chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Preparation:
Cook the jasmine rice ahead of time and let it cool. Day-old rice works best for fried rice as it's drier and less likely to clump together.
Vegetable Prep:
Chop the mixed vegetables into small, uniform pieces.
Cooking Eggs:
Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the beaten eggs and scramble them until just cooked. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
Stir-Fry Vegetables:
In the same pan, add the remaining oil. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender yet still crisp.
Adding Rice:
Add the cooked and cooled rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and mix well with the vegetables.
Sauce and Seasoning:
Pour soy sauce and oyster sauce over the rice and mix thoroughly.
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add a few drops of sesame oil for extra flavor if desired.
Combining Ingredients:
Add the cooked eggs back to the pan and mix them in with the rice and vegetables.
Finishing Touch:
Stir in sliced green onions and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
Garnish and Serve:
Garnish with fresh cilantro or additional chopped green onions if desired.
Serve hot as a standalone dish or as a side with your favorite protein.
